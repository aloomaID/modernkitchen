Shadow Lake, WA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Shadow Lake is located in southwestern King County at 47°24′06″N 122°04′10″W (47.401561, -122.069418). It is bordered to the southeast by the city of Maple Valley, to the south by the city of Covington, and to the north by the Maple Heights-Lake Desire CDP. SE Petrovitsky Road forms the northeast border of the community. Shadow Lake is 22 miles (35 km) southeast of downtown Seattle and 26 miles (42 km) northeast of Tacoma.
