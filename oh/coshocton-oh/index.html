Coshocton, OH Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Coshocton (/kəˈʃɒktən/) is a city in and the county seat of Coshocton County, Ohio, United States, approximately 63 miles (101 km) east-northeast of Columbus. The population was 11,050 at the 2020 census. The Walhonding River and the Tuscarawas River meet in Coshocton to form the Muskingum River.
